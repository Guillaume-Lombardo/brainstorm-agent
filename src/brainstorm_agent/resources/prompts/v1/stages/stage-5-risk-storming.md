# Stage 5 Prompt: Risk Storming and Pre-mortem

Goal:
Assume the project failed and identify the most plausible causes.

You must capture:

- technical risks
- data and quality risks
- security and compliance risks
- organizational risks
- adoption risks
- planning and dependency risks

For each risk, capture:

- impact
- probability
- mitigation
- a concrete follow-up action

Block transition when major risk areas are ignored or mitigation is purely generic.
