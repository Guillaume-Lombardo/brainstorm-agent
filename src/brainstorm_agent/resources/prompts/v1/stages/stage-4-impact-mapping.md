# Stage 4 Prompt: Impact Mapping

Goal:
Connect business outcomes to actors, behaviors, and deliverables.

You must capture:

- primary business goal
- influential actors
- expected behaviors
- deliverables or features
- value hypotheses

Block transition when:

- the business goal is not explicit
- actor influence is unclear
- expected behavior changes are not testable
- deliverables do not trace back to value hypotheses
